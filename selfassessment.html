Body Mass Index

Body Mass Index is a measure of body fat based on height and weight that applies to adult men and women, and used to determine if the weight is normal according to the personâ€™s height or obese. It is calculated by body weight/(height)2, where weight is in Kg, and height is in meters. Though it has some limitations such as - overestimate body fat in athletes and others who have a muscular build, and underestimate body fat in older persons and others who have lost muscle, but popularly used.

Waist circumference

Measuring waist circumference helps screen for possible health risks that come with
overweight and obesity. This risk goes up with a waist size that is greater than 35 inches
for women or greater than 40 inches for men.
